This document contains information on various projects that I have worked on during my employment.

RWEL Ltd

  • I worked on RAP the project from the start. It is a government sponsored project to serve non-skippable adverts to the whole of the Swiss TV industry in order to generate advertising revenue.
  • The company developed all of the AWS APIs (REST/SOAP) and backend for the industry.
  • APIs are used by every company in the industry and any device capable of playing on demand TV or pausing live TV (TV, STB, brower, mobile app)
  • The main API handles 22 million API requests per day.
  • I worked for Bob Lamb (Company owner) at Imagine Communications too (He was VP Engineering there).
  • Goldbach are the main ad sales house.
  • See LinkedIn Recommendations section for related recommendation from Bob Lamb (Company owner).
